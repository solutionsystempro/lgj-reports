Closing Approach

Discovery-first · no pressure · high conviction
🧠 Discovery Framework
Uses a structured Situation → Problem → Data → Rationale discovery process to establish pain before presenting a solution. Prospects articulate their own gap — making the close a natural conclusion rather than a push.
"When the prospect names their own problem in their own words, they're already halfway sold."
💬 Two-Truths Questioning
Opens by affirming what's working for the prospect, then asks what they'd improve. Eliminates ego defensiveness and gets prospects talking honestly about their gaps — without any confrontation or pressure.
"What would you want to improve about your lead gen if you could?"
🎭 Tone Over Technique
Calm, curious, non-reactive energy closes more deals than any script. The 90%+ close rate isn't built on objection-handling — it's built on the prospect feeling no pressure to resist in the first place. Reviewed weekly with a sales coach.
📊 Rational Doubt Creation
Challenges the status quo without pressure by asking prospects to justify their current strategy:
"Why not just double down on what's already working for you?"
Forces the prospect to articulate the ceiling of their current approach — they sell themselves on change.
