Ian didn't just close deals. He changed the economics of the entire offer.

Removed the 40% Discount
Convinced the founder to stop discounting the core offer.
The program was being sold at a 40% discount on the website. Ian made the case to sell at full price. The discount came down. Every sale after that point was worth more, for every closer on the team.
Interest on Payment Plans
Started charging interest on payment plans company-wide.
Payment plans were being offered at face value, leaving revenue on the table. Ian introduced interest charges on installments. Again, a structural change that improved every future transaction.
