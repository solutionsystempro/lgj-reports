The Journey

1999 to Now - 25+ Years of Building, Losing, and Coming Back
Act 1 - The Builder (1999-2012)

Built from scratch. Then built bigger.

Started with cold calls and VHS tapes: a 5-figure monthly wakeboarding school built through pure hustle. Moved into real estate and became a top producer. Then went all in on MLM: 2,500 reps, $500K+ MRR, a full-scale organization.

Then a single executive dispute made it disappear. Twice.

Then it was gone overnight.
Act 2 - The Mountains (2012-2024)

14 years. On purpose.

Tandem pilot. Snowboard instructor. Airbnb arbitrage operator (covered all living expenses). Lyft and Uber driver when weather grounded the flights.

"When the conditions didn't let me fly people for money, I drove people for money. I always found a way to cash flow."

The entrepreneurs who outlast everyone understand who they are before they sell anything. Those 14 years were the education no funnel can teach.

Act 3 - Growth Operator (2024-Now)

All of it, applied.

$550K closed. 218 clients. AI tools built from scratch. Not just closing. Building the whole machine.

The 25 years of pattern recognition, the resilience from losing everything twice, and the systems thinking from cash-flowing life in the mountains: all of it running in parallel on every call.
